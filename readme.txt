    9 An automated tool that uses **Google Gemini 2.5 Flash** to transfer
      gemstone appearances onto ring models. This project allows users to
      batch-process multiple ring designs with various gemstone references to
      create photorealistic product images.
   10
   11 ## ‚ú® Features
   12
   13 - **Folder Upload Support:** Drag and drop entire folders of ring models
      and stone references.
   14 - **Batch Processing:** Automatically generates all possible combinations
      of rings and stones.
   15 - **High Resolution:** Outputs photorealistic 1024x1024 studio-quality
      images.
   16 - **Flexible Input:** Supports both local file uploads and image URLs.
   17 - **Customizable Prompts:** Fine-tune the AI output with custom
      instructions.
   18 - **Real-time Progress:** Track generation progress through a dedicated
      Gradio interface.
   19

   27 ### Installation

   2 ## üõ†Ô∏è Usage
   3
   4 1. Run the script:
     python NewFile_1.py


    1 2. Open the Gradio interface in your browser (usually
      `http://localhost:7860`).
    2 3. **Upload Ring Models:** Upload the base ring designs you want to use.
    3 4. **Upload Stone References:** Upload images of the stones/textures you

    4 5. **Configure:** Set the number of variations per combination and any
      custom prompts.
    5 6. **Generate:** Click "Generate All Combinations" and wait for the
      results in the `generated_rings/` folder.

    7 ## üìä How it Works
    8
    9 The system utilizes the **Gemini 2.5 Flash Image** model. It takes two
      images as input:
   10 1. **Image 1 (Ring Model):** The structural base.
   11 2. **Image 2 (Stone Reference):** The color and pattern source.
   12
   13 The AI performs a style transfer, preserving the ring's geometry while
      updating the center stone to match the reference, all while maintaining a
      consistent photorealistic studio aesthetic on a pure white background.
